About Lacrosse

Lacrosse is a fast-paced team sport. Played with a lacrosse stick and ball, it is one of the oldest sports in North America made by the native americans as a war game.

Equipment For Lacrosse Players

  1. Lacrosse Stick
    • Attack/Midfield Stick
    • Defense Stick
    • Goalie Stick
  2. Protective Gear
    • Helmet
    • Gloves
    • Pads (Shoulder and Arm)
  3. Footwear
    • Cleats
    • Turf Shoes
